Starter Packs

All New players can recieve a Starter Pack, this is a box containing a few items this is to give the player a start on our server where alot of players have large bases and are well established with materials and humanity. To get a Starter Pack just find a place to build your base according to the server rules and ask an Admin on sidechat for the Starter Pack, they will get you one when then can.




Hints

  • 1: Place the Supplies into a Safe as the Kit will despawn on Server Restart OR if you relog into the game

  • 2: Server has Snap & Vector Building to help you - view tutorials on how to use them

  • 3: Bases are Deleted if unused for more than 10 days - Maintain your base every 10 days

New Players can get a Starter kit

Missions

Missions run on our servers - These contain AI and have various tasks to complete, Once you kill the AI you can recover the reward from the weapon boxes, each different mission can have a different reward - The Harder the mission the higher the reward.

Loot can range from weapons, Medical supplies, Food, Building supplies, Money and some rare items.

When a mission runs its is broadcast on screen in game and marked by a colored circle on your map, The different colors denote the skill levels of the AI and difficulty of that mission.


Green

Treasure Hunt Missions - Green Circle on Map ,No Ai at these, Treasure may be any where inside the circle

Low Grade Loot Food Medical Building Ammo

Blue

Uses our old Mission System (highly modified Ai behaviour), Ai skill ranges between 2 and 3 moderate.

Shot Ai can gain or loose you 200 Humanity depending if your a Hero or a Bandit. Medium Grade Loot. Most Mission Vehicles will despawn on server restart. All Mission Vehicles can be Sold at traders.

Red

Uses our old Mission System (highly modified Ai behaviour), Ai skill ranges between 3 and 4 Hard to Hardest on Server.

Shot Ai can gain or loose you 200 Humanity depending if your a Hero or a Bandit. High End Loot. Most Mission Vehicles will despawn on server restart. All Mission Vehicles can be Sold at traders.

Other interactions


View Distance

Players can adjust their own View Distance in game. The player needs either Binoculars, Rangefinders or night vision goggles in their gear to be able to do so. Open Gear and Right click on either one of those, then select the Distance that you want to view to. Remember the higher you set the View Distance the lower the players FPS will be.

Locate Vehicles

Required items: Toolbelt map
By pressing "Locate Vehicles" located at right-clicking the toolbelt item map.
The game searches the your inventory for any keys in your toolbelt slots. ( This progress can takeup a few seconds ).
When the process is complete, it shows the Vehicle(s) that belongs to the key(s) on the map with an orange dot.
